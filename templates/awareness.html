Cardiac Awareness

Understanding heart health, symptoms, and preventive measures can significantly reduce the risk of cardiac diseases.

Common Heart Attack Symptoms

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in arms, neck, jaw, or back
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Unusual fatigue or nausea

Healthy Diet Tips

  • Eat fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Reduce salt, sugar, and saturated fats
  • Avoid processed and fried foods
  • Drink enough water daily
  • Limit alcohol consumption

Exercise & Lifestyle Tips

  • Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily
  • Practice yoga or meditation to reduce stress
  • Avoid smoking and tobacco products
  • Maintain a healthy sleep routine
