Cardiac Awareness

Understanding heart health, symptoms, and preventive measures can significantly reduce the risk of cardiac diseases.

Heart Attack Symptoms

These symptoms may appear suddenly or build gradually. Early recognition can save lives.

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in arms or shoulders
  • Pain in neck, jaw, or back
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Unusual fatigue or nausea

Healthy Diet Guidelines

A heart-friendly diet helps control blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar levels.

  • Fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Lean proteins and healthy fats
  • Limit salt and sugar intake
  • Avoid processed and fried foods
  • Drink sufficient water daily
  • Moderate alcohol consumption

Preventive Measures

Adopting a healthy lifestyle significantly lowers the risk of heart disease.

  • Exercise at least 30 minutes daily
  • Maintain a healthy body weight
  • Quit smoking completely
  • Manage stress effectively
  • Get regular health checkups
